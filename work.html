Distributed digital library system of EMERCOM

Digital library system

Alee Software, 2015

Legend:

Russian Federation Ministry of Emergencies faced with the problem of storing and structuring publication funds of the 12 distributed departments. The mission was to build an information system that would provide 24/7 access to the collections of libraries, safety and relevance of data and uninterrupted communications between departments. Alee Software won the tender for this project. I was chosen as one of the project managers.

Project activity:

  • Information architecture modeling
  • Project management (4 developers, 2 testers, 1 graphic designer)
  • Modules "FAQ and help", "Synchronization", "Reports": UX design, prototyping, specification writing
  • Closing testing
